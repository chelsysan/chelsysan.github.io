Indoor Waterpark?

I don't know why, but I instantly change into a swimsuit and carefully enter the black tunnel. Water rushes past me as I slide down and end up catapulting into a large pool, where other people where relaxing and just having fun. Returning to the shabby platform, I overlook an amateurly built indoor waterpark from a great height. The slides definitely aren't sturdy, but my sister and nieces insist on sliding down the tubes with me over and over again.
